About

I am a data analyst with a rare combination of technical analytical skills and a proven ability to communicate insights and lead projects within and across different teams. I am able to combine my natural curiosity of social issues with well-designed analyses, thoughtful design, and powerful visualizations to generate insight. The wise use of these skills can provide a deeper understanding of issues like urban public education, housing and income inequality, and public health.

I earned a BA in Music from Truman State University and an MA and MPA from Indiana University - Bloomington. Currently, I work as a Data Analyst for Ingenuity, a Chicago-based arts education think tank, enabling data-driven decisions and policymaking in the nation's third-largest school district. By combining data collection and analysis with advocacy and strategy, we serve as the backbone organization for Chicago's arts and cultural community.
